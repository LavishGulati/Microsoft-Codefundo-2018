Mission

In a world where climate change and global warming are becoming more and more commonplace, we aim to provide a means to coordinate and allocate resources for effective disaster management. We aim to utilize technology as a means to improve the quality of lives of people living in vulnerable areas.

About the website

The recent Kerala and Uttarakhand floods and their devastating impact on society make natural disasters appear as unstoppable beasts. While they may be more deadly than before, in most cases lives are lost due to lack of contigency plans. By using modern day technology, natural disasters can be tamed and their footprint minimized. This website is a stepping stone in the direction of assembling a coordinated response and stopping natural disasters in their tracks.
